Greek Yoghurt

Especially Greek Yoghurt with berries.

Greek yogurt is a thick, creamy dairy product made by straining regular yogurt to remove excess whey, resulting in a richer texture and higher protein content. It has a tangy flavor and is often used in both sweet and savory dishes. Greek yogurt is known for its health benefits, including aiding digestion, supporting muscle growth, and being a good source of calcium and probiotics. It's a versatile and nutritious option for snacks, breakfasts, or as a healthier substitute in recipes.
